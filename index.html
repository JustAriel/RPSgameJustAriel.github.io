How to play?

Rock-paper-scissors is a simple hand game that is played by two people. The game is usually played with the players counting to three in unison and simultaneously throwing one of three hand signs representing rock, paper, or scissors.
The winner is determined by the rules:

-Rock beats scissors
-Scissors beat paper
-Paper beats rock
